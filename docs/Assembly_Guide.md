# Keyther assembling guide

1. Prepare all the components and heat up your soldering iron.
   
   > NOTE: It is strongly recommended to flash the firmware to the microcontroller before proceeding to the second step

2. Start by soldering the pin strips to the microcontroller, helping yourself with the PCB to position the shorter pins side as perpendicular to the microcontroller as possible.

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://github.com/MagoSaronno/keyther/blob/main/docs/images/WABP_soldered_up.jpg" width="700"/>
</p>

3. Place the PCB with the **bottom side** facing up and solder all the hot-swap sockets as shown in the video below: 

 <p align="center"> 
   <a href="https://youtu.be/L0AAVJCXs2I">
    <img src="https://img.youtube.com/vi/L0AAVJCXs2I/0.jpg" alt="Watch the video" width="700"/>
  </a>
</p>

- tin one of the two PCB pads with a moderate amount of solder;
   
- place the socket in the correct position;
   
- place the soldering iron tip inside the socket contact above the tinned pad and press down until the socket is properly soldered to the pad;
   
- solder the other socket contact.

> I suggest starting to solder the right sockets pad from the side opposite to your dominant hand. Then, rotate the board by 180Â° and solder the other pads. 

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://github.com/MagoSaronno/keyther/blob/main/docs/images/hotswap_soldered.jpg" width="700"/>
</p>

4- Place the microcontroller underneath the PCB by inserting the pins as shown in the image below. Then, solder it to the board and trim the pins.

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://github.com/MagoSaronno/keyther/blob/main/docs/images/microcontroller_position.jpg" width="700"/>
</p>

5- Place the RJ45 socket underneath the PCB in the correct holes and solder it to the board.

<p align="center">
    <img src="https://github.com/MagoSaronno/keyther/blob/main/docs/images/RJ45_position.jpg" width="700"/>
</p>

6- Insert the switches in their sockets and add the keycaps.

7- Enjoy your Keyther!
